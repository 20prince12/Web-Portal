Frequently Asked Questions

Q.What is this portal about?

This portal is about bringing innovative minds together.And providing them an oppurtunity to work on their idea and make their dreams succeed with the help of government and other private institutions.

Q.How often do the admins review your idea?

There is no specific time period for the admin to review/accept or decline your idea.But there will be a team dedicated to review your ideas. So,your idea will be reviewed as soon as possible.

Q.Is there any payment option included in this portal?

No,there is no payment option included in this site. It is free for everyone.

Q.How can I get a patent on my idea?

We are not providing the option patent your idea. But, we are going to provide the basic information about where and how you can get your own patent.

Q.How does this portal work?

This portal has a simple UI with multi-lingual option. Which makes it easier for all the users to work.
He or she can register into he portal with some simple steps and submit their idea after logging into the portal. Which will be later reviewed by the officials. Who will either accept or decline and can write remarks about the particular idea. And the user can track the status of his idea by using the given id dedicated to the idea.

Q.What if I feel someone copied my idea?

We take idea infringement very seriously. If you cantact us through an email with the subject regarding "Similar idea". With including the id given to your idea and the idea which you feel is copied including with the date and time of submission of your idea. We will go through it and contact you as earlier as possible.
