What is this Website?

Welcome to The Official Website of Ideas, In recent times there is an increasing trend of Entrepreneurship in India. Everyone has their own ideas and want to build a business on the idea but there is no proper platform to share ideas and get help to turn the idea into business. AICTE which is responsible for technical education in India want to change the scenario by creating a portal which will gather innovative ideas. Through this portal AICTE wants to enable relation between start-ups and well-known industries. AICTE already has a portal for their regular responsibilities and functioning but now they want a separate portal for entrepreneurs to share their ideas and connect with industries as well as government. .


Users reach the idea submission portal with the specified URL. A new user can register with their email and other details. A registered user can then login to submit ideas


How Does The Portal Work



After logging into the portal, users can enter the details about their innovative idea and a brief description of the same. User can also upload images videos and research publications regarding the idea. The portal also allows domain experts to register themselves and maintains the list of reviewers in various domains. Once the idea Submission is complete with all the required documents, the details are stored in the portal database. Using the details of domain reviewers, nomination of Domain expert panel is done by the Admin. This Nominated Panel scrutinizes the idea and submits the results in the portal. The results are notified to the user. The IPR documents are generated by the portal with a predefined set of options.
